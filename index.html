Description

Super Mario Galaxy was released in 2007 to the Wii. It was the third 3D game in Nintendo's Super Mario series. The game is set in out space, where Mario travels to different galaxies and planets to collect Power Stars by defeating enemys and completing quests. Can you save the universe from Bowser and rescue Princess Peach? Or will the Galaxy Remain without a hero forever?
